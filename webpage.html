The SRC for the IMG tag can be any URL on the internet, but URLs can change from time to time, so it's often better to do the following:

  1. Download the picture to your laptop.
  2. Optionally, rename it to something concise and memorable.
  3. Copy the picture to your project folder on the server.
  4. Put the filename of the picture as the URL of the image.

You might need to use the chmod command, as needed, to ensure that the permissions for the files and subfolders of your project folder are okay.
